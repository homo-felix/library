Hi there,

I am JL, I've opened this website today, 20th April 2020. I named it "library", because I happened to capture a nice photo of a library on my way home, an hour ago-- I like this photo very much, the weather was so good. Since I didn't know which name should my new website get, I simply grasped the word on this photo: Bibliothek, in English means: library.

I'll show you this photo later.

Well, funny name, isn't it? Funny the way you write these words, too-- you might say. Strange people who's created a strange website, write some stupid things here.

It's not about a library really, I've got no idea what am I going to write, nor what this stuff is going to look like.. It's nothing important, though. For me it's one of my projekts; a place to kill time; where I can use some English (or any other freak languages), write some sort of public diaries or anything, practice some newly learned programming skills and so on.

And this time it would be a simple website, as simple as it could be. Simple and unique. (Hauptsache, einfach für mich zu schreiben und für andere zu lesen. Is that not a great idea?)

Of course there could also be things that no one could unterstand, too. Why not? That's funny as well. I'm just going to write whatever I like. Perhaps I'll let everybody be able to do that here, too.

I suppose you would keep reading, right?
